VHH For Algae

Antibodies are widely used as reagents in biomedicine both for research and dianostics.

However the availability dedicated to algae study and detection is limited, not to mention that cheap and reliable antibodies are not available.

The understanding of algal biology and the monitoring of their blooms are great challenges that can be addressed from different perspectives.

For this reason unicellular algae are the perfect target in the development of new strategies based on recombinant nanobodies.
