Let's work together!

As experts on antibody biotechnology,
we are always looking for new challenges for their application:

  • further validation with a wider array of targets
  • implementation into monitoring schemes
  • development of reagents for the detection of new targets

If you are interested in testing...

... one of our nanobodies with your strain of Alexandrium spp, we are very happy to collaborate!

If you have a new target...

... and you are interested in engeneering nanobodies against it, we can start a project together!
